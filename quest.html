questjs 0.1

is a retro experiment to emulate the 80's adventure games, where you had to write the commands yourself to solve each room in the adventure.
Enter the Commands in the blue strip just above.
In this box you will see the description of each room. Above you will see an image of the room (somtimes there are clues there).
Right to the image is the history of your commands and the outcome of each command.
To see a list of available commands write "words" or "dic" or "dictionary". Also, You have to write "start" to start.
